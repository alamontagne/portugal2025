My Portugal Adventure 2025

Hi! This is your fun guide to Portugal, starting in Lisbon. Let’s explore! 🇵🇹

Save me to your iPad home screen for offline use!

Getting Around 🚆

Train Stations in Lisbon:

  • Oriente: For Sintra or Algarve
  • Rossio: For Sintra, Óbidos, Nazaré
  • Cais do Sodré: For Belém, Estoril, Cascais

Tickets:

  • Viva Viagem Card: For buses, trams, metro (reloadable!)
  • Lisboa Card: Covers transit + entry to cool places (buy at Rossio Station, $42/72h)
  • Buy at: Green ABEP kiosk (bottom of Praça dos Restauradores)

Day 1: Monday, March 10 - Lisbon Fun Begins! 🏙️

Where: Baixa District & Chiado

  • Take the Metro ($1.50) to Baixa
  • Check into Hotel Pestana Rua Augusta
  • Walk Rua Augusta (no cars allowed!)
  • Museu Núcleo Arqueológico (10 AM–6 PM, Mon-Sat)
  • Ride Elevador de Santa Justa (7:30 AM–9 PM) for a cool view! 🎡
  • See Museu Carmo (10 AM–6 PM, Mon-Sat) - old ruins!
  • Eat at Café Confeitaria Nacional 🍰
  • Walk Rossio Square & Praça dos Restauradores (big statue!)
  • Stroll Avenida da Liberdade
  • Chiado: National Museum of Contemporary Art (10 AM–6 PM, Tue-Sun)
  • Igreja de São Roque (1 hour, pretty church! ⛪)
  • Miradouro de São Pedro de Alcântara (awesome view)
  • Jardim do Príncipe Real (chill park)

Dinner: Fado music at O Faia (book ahead! 🎶)

Dessert: Alice Gelato near the hotel 🍦

Day 2: Tuesday, March 11 - Alfama Adventure! 🏰

Where: Alfama & Parque das Nações

  • Breakfast at Pastelaria Santo António ☕
  • Castelo de São Jorge (9 AM–9 PM, 1 hour) - castle views! 👑
  • Walk Alfama: São Vicente de Fora church (10 AM–6 PM, Tue-Sun)
  • Portas do Sol Square (pretty spot)
  • Sé Cathedral (10 AM–6 PM, Mon-Sat, free!)
  • Museu Nacional do Azulejo (tile museum, closed Mon)
  • Lunch: Chapitô à Mesa, A Baiuca, or Taberna Moderna
  • Buy 3-day Lisboa Card at Rossio Station
  • Red Line to Parque das Nações
  • Oceanário (2 hours, fish everywhere! 🐠)
  • Pavilion of Knowledge (science fun!)
  • Vasco da Gama Mall (shopping! 🛍️)

Dinner: Restaurante D’Bacalhau

Day 3: Wednesday, March 12 - Belém Blast! ⛪

Where: Belém District (Lisboa Card)

  • Monument to the Discoveries (famous explorers!)
  • Belém Tower (10 AM–5:30 PM, Tue-Sun) - UNESCO site! 🌍
  • Eat at Pastéis de Belém (custard tarts, 8 AM–11 PM) 🍮
  • Lunch: A Padaria Portuguesa or Floresta de Belém
  • Praça do Império (big park)
  • Jerónimos Monastery (10 AM–5:30 PM, Tue-Sun, $10) - amazing building!
  • Palácio da Ajuda (fancy palace + garden)
  • Jardim Botânico da Ajuda (pretty plants 🌸)
  • Museu Nacional de Arte Antiga (10 AM–6 PM, Tue-Sun, 2 hours) - coffee by the river!
  • MAAT (11 AM–7 PM, Wed-Mon) - climb the roof for free!
  • LX Factory (cool shops, Ler Devagar bookshop 📚)

Day 4: Thursday, March 13 - Sintra Magic! 🏰✨

Where: Sintra (leave hotel at 7:30 AM)

  • 8:30 AM train from Lisbon (40 min, Lisboa Card)
  • Bus 434 to Palácio Nacional da Pena (9:30 AM–11:30 AM, 2 hours) - red & yellow palace!
  • Walk to Moorish Castle (11:30 AM–1 PM, 1.5 hours) - old fortress!
  • Lunch in town (1 PM–2 PM)
  • Quinta da Regaleira (2 PM–5 PM, 10 min walk) - secret gardens & well! 🌿
  • Palácio Nacional de Sintra (5 PM–6:30 PM, 1 hour) - town palace
  • Back to Lisbon by 6:30 PM train

Day 5: Friday, March 14 - Cascais Coast! 🌊

Where: Cascais & Nearby

  • Train to Cascais (Cais do Sodré, Lisboa Card)
  • Santa Marta Lighthouse
  • Boca do Inferno (cave beaches, 1.5 km walk) 🏖️
  • Cabo da Roca (edge of Europe!)
  • Palácio de Monserrate (9 AM–7 PM, 1.5–2 hours) - bus 435 or taxi

Day 6: Saturday, March 15 - Art & Parks! 🎨

Where: Lisbon

  • Calouste Gulbenkian Museum (Wed-Mon) - best art!
  • Avenida Novas: Shopping + Parque Eduardo VII view
  • Jardim Botânico (10 AM–5 PM) 🌳
  • Parque Eduardo VII (big park!)
  • Embaixada Mall (fancy shopping 🛍️)
  • Palácio Nacional de Queluz (train to Queluz-Belas, 1 km walk, Lisboa Card)

Dinner: Pink Street or Mercado de Ribeira (Time Out)

Day 7: Sunday, March 16 - Relax & Explore! 🌳

Where: Lisbon

  • São Roque Church & Museum (9 AM–7 PM) ⛪
  • Jardim de São Pedro de Alcântara
  • Jardim Zoológico de Lisboa (zoo time! 🦒)
  • Fronteira Palace & Garden

Alt Option: LX Factory Farmers Market (9:30 AM–4 PM)

Day 8: Monday, March 17 - Last Morning! ✈️

Where: Lisbon (until noon)

  • Breakfast at Café A Brasileira ☕
  • Lisbon Story Centre (opens 10 AM)
  • Shop: Avenida da Liberdade or Chiado (world’s oldest bookstore! 📚)
  • Museu Nacional do Azulejo (tile museum)
  • Head to the airport
